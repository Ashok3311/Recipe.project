Delicious Breakfast Biryani Recipe

This flavorful Breakfast Biryani is the perfect way to start your day with a twist.
Made with aromatic spices, rice, and fresh vegetables, it's a delightful morning treat.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Enjoy your homemade Biryani!
