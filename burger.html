Burger Recipe

This is a mouthwatering homemade burger recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 beef patty
  • 1 burger bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup and mustard

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan.
  2. Cook the beef patty to your desired doneness.
  3. Toast the burger bun.
  4. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato slices, onion slices, cheese, ketchup, and mustard.
  5. Place the cooked patty on the bun.
  6. Top with the other half of the bun.
  7. Serve and enjoy!

Enjoy your homemade burger!
