Cocktail Recipe

This is a refreshing cocktail recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 1 oz triple sec
  • 1 oz cranberry juice
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • Ice cubes
  • Lime wheel for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a shaker with vodka, triple sec, cranberry juice, and lime juice.
  2. Add ice cubes to the shaker.
  3. Shake well until chilled.
  4. Strain the cocktail into a chilled martini glass.
  5. Garnish with a lime wheel.
  6. Serve and enjoy!

Enjoy your refreshing cocktail!
