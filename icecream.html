Ice Cream Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • Flavorings (e.g., chocolate chips, fruit, nuts)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine heavy cream, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract. Stir until sugar is fully dissolved.
  2. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and follow the manufacturer's instructions for churning.
  3. Add your choice of flavorings during the last few minutes of churning.
  4. Transfer the churned ice cream to a lidded container and freeze for several hours until firm.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

Enjoy your homemade ice cream!
