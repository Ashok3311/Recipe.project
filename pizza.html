Pizza Recipe

This is a delicious homemade pizza recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  6. Add the sliced pepperoni, bell peppers, and black olives.
  7. Sprinkle dried oregano over the toppings.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
